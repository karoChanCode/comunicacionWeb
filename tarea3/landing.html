Activities

Let your furry friends run wild in the great outdoors. This activity is all about play and socializing. We'll head to the local dog-friendly park, where your dogs can mingle and make new friends. They'll enjoy hours of fetch, chase, and sniffing adventures in a safe and enclosed environment. It's the perfect opportunity for your dogs to burn off energy and bond with other canine companions.

Get ready for some pawsitively cute and hilarious moments as your dogs strut their stuff in their favorite costumes! Whether it's a superhero, a movie character, or a classic pumpkin costume, our doggie costume contest is all about creativity and fun. Prizes will be awarded for the most original, funniest, and cutest costumes. So, dress up your pup, and let's have a howling good time!

Treat your dogs to a delicious and visually appealing activity. We'll provide freshly baked pupcakes and a variety of dog-friendly toppings like peanut butter frosting, bacon bits, and shredded cheese. Your dogs will have a blast decorating their very own pupcakes. Not only is it entertaining, but it's also a tasty way for them to enjoy a special treat. Plus, they get to take their decorated pupcakes home as a yummy souvenir!
